# Narrative and Progression

## End-state

The player should not arrive at omniscience.

The player should arrive at disciplined judgment.

That means the campaign arc is not "mystery solved forever." It is a movement from confidence, through contradiction, toward stewardship.

## Eight-phase progression model

### 1. Trust

The world appears interpretable. Patterns hold. Small victories teach confidence.

### 2. Patterning

The player develops heuristics and begins solving larger structures more efficiently.

### 3. Strain

Some systems consume effort without obvious payoff. Doubt appears.

### 4. Commitment

The player must increasingly decide which processes deserve time, access, and risk.

### 5. Reflection

Puzzles begin to echo earlier methods and earlier solutions.

### 6. Contradiction

The world starts defeating general rules the player believed were reliable.

### 7. Self-reference shock

The player encounters structures that appear to react to their way of thinking.

### 8. Acceptance

The player ends not with total control, but with wisdom about where action is meaningful.

## Flagship macro arcs

Use these as campaign-scale throughlines.

### The Broken District

Opening promise arc. The player restores a wounded district and learns the world is lawful and worth touching.

### The Garden That Remembers

Cultivation arc. The player learns that care can reveal truth, but that some growth only entangles.

### The Price of Foresight

Oracle debt arc. The player is tempted to buy partial certainty and learns that prediction is costly.

### The Returning Atlas

Cartography arc. Maps, traces, and representations begin to matter as much as routes themselves.

### The Schism of Stewards

Political arc. Different factions embody incompatible methods for living with unfinished things.

### The Witness Who Learns You

Companion arc. Another being begins to inherit, mirror, or suffer from the player's methods.

### The Margins Start Speaking

Mirror arc. The world begins to quote the player's habits back to them.

### The Unfinished Engine

Legacy arc. The player decides what should be opened, tended, sealed, abandoned, or passed on.

## Contradiction patterns

Contradiction should target methods rather than isolated wrong guesses.

Useful named patterns include:

- Local Victory, Global Spill
- The Ignored Key
- Risk Inversion
- Cartographic Decay
- Misapplied Inheritance
- The Speaking Trace
- False Totality

## Narrative rule

Every major arc should contain:

- a lure
- a local rule
- a cost of commitment
- partial signs of progress
- a possible contradiction
- a downstream consequence that touches another thread

This keeps the game from becoming a chain of disconnected puzzles.
