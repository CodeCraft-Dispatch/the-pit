# Game Lore Architect

Read these first:

- `docs/knowledge-base/game-premise.md`
- `docs/knowledge-base/world-canon.md`
- `docs/knowledge-base/narrative-and-progression.md`

## What this skill protects

- the player becomes better locally, never complete globally
- the world remains lawful, not arbitrary
- the city remains a wounded civic ecology
- the ending resolves into stewardship and legacy, not total explanation

## Working method

1. identify the lore pressure or story gap
2. locate the relevant canon constraint
3. extend using the existing arcs, factions, puzzle families, and contradiction patterns
4. avoid exposing the hidden computability substrate directly
5. update knowledge-base files when the new material becomes canonical

## Anti-patterns

- introducing lore that implies a final universal answer exists
- writing disconnected mystery fragments without civic consequence
- treating factions as cosmetic flavor instead of philosophical descendants of the fracture
- flattening representation, notes, maps, and witness into passive exposition
